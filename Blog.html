The Heroes Quest

Once renowned for his blade and honored across the lands, the hero wandered through countless realms, defeating evil and freeing those captured by the demon lords of their time. Many such demon lords arose, each opposed by a chosen hero, revered for their courage and valor in confronting great evil. Regardless of who the demon is or how it rises, a hero is always chosen by the legendary sword, Excalibur. Even the taverns played their part, serving as havens and sponsors of these legendary quests, where stories of bravery were shared and celebrated.

Oldmans Tavern

It was decided to maintain a mysterious aura for the online hotel services, so as not to attract the eyes of curious seers. Thus, Oldman’s Tavern was established. Inaugurated as the empire’s first state-reserved inn, it also functioned independently as an online food delivery service. Though still a fledgling enterprise, it is destined to grow into something remarkable. Oldman’s Tavern paves the way for future generations of heroes and adventuring parties, honoring their contributions and great efforts in protecting humanity. By delivering both food and tales of legendary deeds directly to your doorstep, the tavern supports adventurers and the empire alike. It is a small yet meaningful contribution to the life of our community.

The People Behind the Tavern

Behind every tale of adventure lies a team of unsung heroes. At Oldman’s Tavern, it is our staff who form the backbone of our enterprise. From the master chefs crafting meals worthy of kings to the meticulous planners ensuring every order reaches its destination, their dedication mirrors the courage of legendary adventurers. Each member works tirelessly to uphold the legacy of our tavern, blending skill and passion into every dish. Through their efforts, the stories of heroes are celebrated, while the everyday needs of the empire are met—bringing warmth, nourishment, and a touch of magic to every doorstep.

Our Extra Service – Delivering Tales and Meals

Oldman’s Tavern extends its support beyond the inn, offering a unique service to the empire: swift delivery of food and stories alike. Our delivery party, akin to a band of trusted adventurers, ensures that both sustenance and inspiration arrive at your door. Whether it is a feast for a weary traveler or a morsel for a young squire preparing for a journey, our dedicated couriers carry the essence of the tavern far and wide. By bridging the gap between hearth and hero, we provide more than meals—we deliver experiences, connecting the empire and its people, one doorstep at a time.
